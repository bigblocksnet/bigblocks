The Big Blocks Network

Big Blocks Network is built on Ethereum PoA consensus. This is a private blockchain supporting many of our in-house DApp(s) such the LikeNFT. The network supports RPC endpoint which enables Metamask wallet to connect to.

Services

Consultancy
If you are considering a blockchain or Web3 strategy for your business, contact us and discuss. This part of service is FREE. Not all system is blockchain suitable and we will evaluate this together with you. If blockchain is right for you and you are confident with our team, we engage!

Step 1: Select the right blockchain, use a public chain or build a new private/permission chain
Step 2: Design the system, what data should be on-chain and what should be off-chain
Step 3: Design the smart contract, data and functions to be implemented in smart contracts
Step 4: Upgrade and scalability plan
Step 5: Come up with an implementation plan
Step 6: Supervise the implementation process
Step 7: Further support (if needed)
